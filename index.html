Full Metal Alchemist

Fullmetal Alchemist is an anime that follows two brothers, Edward and Alphonse Elric, who are on a journey to find the Philosopher's stone to fix their mutilated bodies.

Hunter X Hunter

Hunter X Hunter is a deceptive story that opens as a child's action adventure story but develops into a dissection of the meaning of friendship and an exploration in loss and suffering. It maintains its action adventure skeleton throughout all of this however.

Samurai Champloo

Samurai Champloo is an adventure story that follows three characters Mugen, Jin and Fuu. Fuu is on a journey to find a man with a specific scar who killed her father? (I think), Mugen is simply a wild card figure who turns out to be more than he appears as Jin is a Samurai with no allegiances trying to do something (I don't remember him much).
